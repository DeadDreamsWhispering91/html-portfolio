The Best Movies Acccording to William

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Joker

Haunting performance by Joaquin Phoenix in a gritty, dark 1980s comic book setting

LOTR Trilogy (Extended)

What can I say? An unmirrored accomplishment in bringing a written fantasy world to life at a mindblowing scale. The props, costumes and CGI still look incredible more than 20 years later.

The Sixth Sense

This might seem like an odd choice to some but Haley-Joel Osmont, Toni Collette and Bruce Willis deliver unforgettable performances that put this supernatural thriller into a league of it's own.
